How does this method work?

The first step is to start with a number, say... 20736. Then, align the digits by spacing them on every other cell like this.

_2_0_7_3_6

The second step is to square each of the digits and align them like this.
_2_0_7_3_6
0400490936

The third and hard step is to take every pair of digits that are one digit apart, every pair of digits that are two digits apart, every pair of digits that are three digits apart, and so on, then multiply those, then double the results, then align them like this.
(If you somehow cannot get what it says, I'll show you a visual representation.)

[1 DIGIT APART] 2&0, 0&7, 7&3, 3&6 -> 2*0*2, 0*7*2, 7*3*2, 3*6*2 -> 00, 00, 42, 36
[2 DIGIT APART] 2&7, 0&3, 7&6 -> 2*7*2, 0*3*2, 7*6*2 -> 28, 00, 84
[3 DIGIT APART] 2&3, 0&6 -> 2*3*2, 0*6*2 -> 12, 00
[4 DIGIT APART] 2&6 -> 2*6*2 -> 24
(Any result that is less than 10 will have an extra 0 digit in the left.)

0400490936
_00004236_

___1200___
____24____
